In addition to her collaborations, RIELL recently launched her solo career as a dark pop artist with electronic elements pulled from the EDM and Gaming Scene with her debut EP . She is currently working on her follow up EP "WINTER" set to be released April 2021 through October 2021. Visuals are a huge part of RIELL's artist project, having music videos accompanying all of her solo releases, as well as a special video to be released with the full WINTER EP in October 2021.
