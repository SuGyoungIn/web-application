Polish music duo of friends connected with passion for music. They started developing their sound few years ago with tracks such as 'Savannah' and 'Tropic Love'. Both songs crossed 100 million total plays on NCS YouTube and Spotify. Diviners already performed on some big events like Sunrise Festival - one of the biggest electronic music festivals in Poland (2016/2018). They were headliners on Love Electro Festival in Austria and played many shows beside Don Diablo, Nicky Romero, Dubvision and more. They also made an official remix for Gareth Emery's single 'Hands'. They were supported by Robin Schulz, The Chainsmokers, Dash Berlin, Gareth Emery, Alan Walker, LVNDSCAPE and many more artists on the music scene.
