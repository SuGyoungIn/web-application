Syn Cole is a seasoned, multi-instrumentalist producer who seamlessly merges electronic and melodic influences with consistent commercial elements. 2020 has seen his music regularly connecting with a global audience. His singles have now amassed over 500 Million streams and a whopping 135 million of them in 2019 (Spotify wrapped).
